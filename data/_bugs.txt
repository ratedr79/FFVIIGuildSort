I'd like to be able to use the stats we generate on the Interactive Team Builder page and pass them automically to the function used by the damage calc. But to do that, we're missing information.

As part of the Player Inventory, I'd like to add a new way to record character specifc stats. Each character has a level up to 100 -- but could increase in the future -- that applies base stats. In addition, each character has two growth trees -- character stream and role stream. Both of those provide additional stats. For example, in game, if I look at my level 100 Vincent:

Base Stats:
HP 13820
PATK 1818
MATK 1801
PDEF 121
MDEF 121
HEAL 936

Character Stream
HP +6024
PATK +601
MATK +587
PDEF +0
MDEF +0
HEAL +586

Role Stream
HP +1500
PATK +300
MATK +300
PDEF +0
MDEF +0
HEAL +0

There is also a special bonus from the "Highwind". The Highwind is a collection of artifacts you can level up by collecting weapons, outfits and progressing through the game. As you level up these artifacts, they provide addition bonuses. For example, by maxed out Highwind provides:

Boost HP +36%
Boost PATK +56%
Boost MATK +56%
Boost PDEF +21%
Boost MDEF +21%
Boost Heal +17.1%

In addition, the role stream grants the character additional effects as they progress through the tree. Attackers get addtional damage (ex elemental potency +10%), tanks get additional defens (ex element resistance +20%), Support gets buff/debuff extension and healers get additional heal potency and effects. While the additional buffs are not exactly important at the moment, because we can simplify them in the damage calc, the patk and matk stats may be important. Because as you add weapons to a character they provide patk and matk stats. Those stats are added to your base stats, multiplied by the highwind bonus and used to generate the totals. These are then used to calculate your damage.

So it's almost like we'd need a section for the Highwind boosts, and then per character would need a section for base, character stream and role stream. I would then like to be able to test the values entered to the in game character to be sure we are within a margin of error.

In game, each player can "brand" their weapons. I believe I've mentioed this before. But it's an RNG based stats boost to weapons. This could be additional patk or matk stats on the weapon, causing our match to be slightly off, since we are not recording the branding values players have on thier weapons.

Once we have a way to enter and save this data, the next step would be to wire it into the interactive team builder. But that can happen as a second phase.
